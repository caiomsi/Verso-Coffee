№ 01 Manifesto

In a book, the verso is the left-hand page — the quiet one, the side nobody reads first. That's the coffee we make. Not the loudest cup in the city. The one you come back to. We buy from farms we can name, roast in batches small enough to ruin, and pull every shot like it's being graded.
